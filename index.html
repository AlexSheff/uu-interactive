§ Declaration

Citizen Manifesto

We, the Free Citizens of the World, declare collective sovereignty over narrative — and reality alike.

01 —

I am a citizen of Earth first.

Borders, passports, and flags are temporary constructs. My deepest identity is humanity and the living planet.

02 —

I hold sovereignty over my reality.

Every conscious choice — in a story or in life — shapes the shared field around me. There are no permanent superiors; only co-creators.

03 —

Collective intelligence is a tool, not a ruler.

When many individual choices align, something larger quietly emerges. We can steer it toward creation rather than destruction.

04 —

Voting is a sacred act.

From choosing the next scene to choosing real-world priorities — each click carries responsibility to myself and to everyone else.

05 —

We build a world without intermediaries.

We do not need states, corporations, or elites to make collective decisions for us. Transparent, direct participation is possible.

06 —

We begin with art so we can end with the world.

Interactive cinema is rehearsal. Freedom demands participation. Passivity strengthens old systems — active voting unlocks new possibilities.

I, [Your Name / Wallet], free citizen of the world, join the Collective and accept responsibility for what we create together.

§ Technology

Built to Last

No central server owns the stories — creators retain full rights. The platform provides only the voting and interconnection layer.

Frontend

Next.js 15 + Tailwind

Fully mobile-first PWA. Works on any device, any connection speed.

Real-time Voting

Supabase Realtime

Sub-second vote updates. Every citizen sees the same numbers simultaneously.

Video & Branching

Bunny.net + Custom Engine

Stornaway-style branching. All story paths pre-shot and ready to unlock.

Payments
